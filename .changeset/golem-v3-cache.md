GOLEM//Console v3 — the cache, a classifier-lens over execution. On by default, it classifies every memory access as a Hit or a Miss bit-for-bit against the unit-3 oracle (Harvard I/D caches, 8 Lines × 2 Sets, 4-word blocks, LRU-by-AGE) without ever changing a value or costing a cycle. The CACHE panel spotlights the Line each Step touches — its two Sets, HIT/MISS coloured, an eviction happening on screen — with the other Lines as a warming strip; the `cache` command toggles the lens; the Console narrates the hit/miss boletim at the end of a run; and `load memory_access` plays the signature scene. The last Poxim unit.
